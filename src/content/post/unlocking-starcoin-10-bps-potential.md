Starcoin’s groundbreaking **10 blocks per second (BPS)** architecture is set to redefine how developers create decentralized applications (DApps) while simultaneously making the Web3 experience more accessible for everyone. By combining the speed and security of its **Move-based smart contracts** with the low-barrier entry of **light client synchronization**, Starcoin is building a platform designed to serve both creators and users.

---

### Starcoin 10 BPS: A Dual Advantage for Developers and Users

Starcoin’s 10 BPS isn't just a technical achievement; it's a strategic design choice that benefits all participants in the Web3 ecosystem. It provides lightning-fast tools for developers and a seamless, user-friendly experience for everyday users.

#### A Developer’s Dream

Starcoin’s 10 BPS architecture and Move smart contracts create an ideal platform for developers to build DApps that demand high throughput and security.

* **Lightning-Fast Transactions**: The 10 BPS achieves new block production every 0.1 seconds, ensuring **near-instant transaction confirmations**. This speed, powered by innovations like **FlexiDAG** and **TurboSTM**, empowers developers to build responsive applications like decentralized exchanges (DEXs) and NFT marketplaces with user experiences that rival centralized platforms.
* **Secure by Design**: The Move programming language is a game-changer for developers. Its **resource-oriented** design inherently protects digital assets from vulnerabilities, giving developers peace of mind when building complex, multi-step DeFi protocols or real-time gaming systems.
* **Modular Ecosystem**: The modular architecture allows developers to use reusable Move modules, **speeding up coding and testing**. The network's high throughput ensures rapid deployment and iteration, enabling creators to bring their visions to market faster.

#### Making Web3 Accessible to Everyone

Starcoin is also making it easy for everyday users to join the decentralized revolution. Its light client synchronization technology removes the major barriers to entry common on other blockchains.

* **Your Phone as a Web3 Gateway**: With light client sync, made possible by **resource snapshots, layered Mergesets, and pruning point optimizations**, users can sync with the network in seconds. Instead of downloading a massive amount of data, a device gets a concise, reliable summary of the blockchain, making the network accessible on low-power devices like smartphones.
* **Empowering True Decentralization**: Light clients use **cryptographic proofs** to verify data, ensuring users can control their assets without relying on centralized services. The 10 BPS delivers this **trustless** experience at scale, making decentralization accessible to millions.
* **Global Accessibility**: The low-resource requirements and high throughput enable broader adoption, fostering **financial inclusion** and new economic opportunities for billions who can join Web3 with just a smartphone.
