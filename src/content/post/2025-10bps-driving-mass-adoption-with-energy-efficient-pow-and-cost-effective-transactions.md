## Energy-Efficient PoW: A Green Blockchain Pioneer

Traditional PoW blockchains, like Bitcoin, are often criticized for their high energy consumption due to intensive computational demands. Starcoin redefines PoW with an optimized consensus mechanism tailored for efficiency through its 10 BPS architecture. By producing a new block every 0.1 seconds, Starcoin maximizes transaction throughput, processing more transactions per unit of energy. The integration of FlexiDAG, a Directed Acyclic Graph structure, and TurboSTM, a parallel transaction execution system, streamlines block production and validation, minimizing redundant computations and slashing energy waste.

Picture a blockchain where transactions are confirmed swiftly with a fraction of the environmental impact. Starcoin’s 10 BPS delivers this, offering a greener alternative that upholds PoW’s security and decentralization while aligning with global sustainability goals. This efficiency is central to Starcoin 2.0’s vision, which emphasizes eco-friendly advancements in blockchain mining.
