By 2009, a new income stream had entered the digital scene, marked by Bitcoin’s “Genesis Block.” Years later, another pacesetter in the **Proof-of-Work (PoW)** sector is making a game-changing enhancement to cryptocurrency mining within the **Move** ecosystem.

**Starcoin**, the first PoW public chain based on Move, recently launched an upgrade to **Starcoin Move7** as part of our new phase, **Starcoin 2.0**.

With **Move7**, Starcoin enables compatibility with mainstream Move dApps, allowing developers to **effortlessly migrate** existing dApps to Starcoin 2.0 **without** needing to rewrite code.

For developers, this provides **greater flexibility and control** over their projects. Below, we explore how this compatible public chain upgrade positions crypto miners to benefit as a core part of Starcoin’s PoW ecosystem:

## 1. Increased Token Utility

An interconnected ecosystem elevates the value of mining rewards—given as native tokens. This occurs because:
- **Utility** of native tokens grows as more dApps rely on them for transactions, gas fees, and staking.
- **Demand** for the tokens rises, potentially driving higher market valuation.

## 2. Higher Transaction Volume

As dApps migrate onto the **Starcoin framework**, the network stands to see:
- **Significant increase** in transaction volume.
- More transactions for miners to process, leading to **higher block rewards** for successfully mined blocks.

## 3. Multiple Income Strategies

By supporting mainstream Move dApps, tokens earned from mining can be used across multiple chains for:
- **DeFi applications** such as governance, yield farming, or contributory roles.
- **Staking or trading** on different platforms, diversifying a miner’s revenue options.

## 4. Future-Proofing PoW Relevance

With Starcoin’s **Move7** upgrade:
- **PoW mining remains viable** in a modern, utility-based chain environment.
- Miners maintain **relevance and profitability** as the blockchain landscape evolves.

## Conclusion

Starcoin’s approach empowers network participants and **distributes value** based on merit. Our commitment to **interoperability** and continuous upgrades not only enriches the ecosystem but also reinforces the true principles of the **Proof-of-Work** model.

Join us in realizing a sustainable ecosystem that preserves the integrity and opportunity of PoW mining, enabling a **21st century income stream** for contributors worldwide.
