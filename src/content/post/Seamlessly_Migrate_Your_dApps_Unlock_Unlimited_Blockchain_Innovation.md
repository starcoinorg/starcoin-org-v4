This compatibility significantly lowers the technical barriers between different blockchains.

For many developers, cross-chain migration typically requires substantial time and resources to adapt to a new environment. Starcoin 2.0 eliminates this obstacle by allowing developers to focus on their projects and deliver better user experiences, **without needing to learn a new language or programming model**.
