In the ever-evolving world of blockchain, Starcoin has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology to ensure robust, scalable, and secure blockchain solutions. Over the past three years, our commitment to infrastructure R&D has culminated in the groundbreaking launch of Starcoin 2.0, known as “Tempus.”

## The Changing Times

“Tempus,” derived from Latin meaning “time,” symbolizes our dedication to continuous innovation and progress. This phase represents a significant leap in overcoming the scalability limitations of traditional PoW consensus while retaining our core principles of decentralization.

## Starcoin 2.0: The Scalable Network

Starcoin 2.0 introduces major upgrades with FlexiDAG and TurboSTM technologies, achieving remarkable improvements:
- **High Transaction Throughput:** FlexiDAG enhances throughput by 17 times, enabling efficient parallel block generation.
- **TurboSTM:** This multi-threaded in-memory computing engine significantly boosts smart contract execution, supporting complex applications like DeFi and NFTs.

## Why Focus on Infrastructure Innovation?

Starcoin’s commitment to deepening infrastructure innovation stems from the necessity to overcome the inherent limitations of traditional PoW systems. By enhancing scalability and transaction speed, Starcoin aims to provide a robust and efficient platform that supports the growing demands of the digital economy. This focus on foundational improvements ensures that the blockchain can handle more complex applications and higher transaction volumes, paving the way for broader adoption and practical use cases.

## Starcoin’s Commitment to Performance

Central to our mission is maximizing performance while maintaining security and decentralization. The introduction of FlexiDAG and TurboSTM in Starcoin 2.0 has led to a tenfold increase in block production speed and a 17-fold improvement in transaction processing capacity. These advancements position Starcoin at the forefront of blockchain technology, ready to meet future demands.

## The Future of Starcoin

Starcoin 2.0 is a significant milestone in creating a scalable, secure, and decentralized digital ecosystem. The “Tempus” phase reflects our ongoing commitment to innovation, aiming to provide a robust infrastructure for Web3 applications and support the growth of the digital economy. This continuous improvement positions Starcoin as a leader in blockchain technology, dedicated to driving forward the capabilities and applications of decentralized systems.
