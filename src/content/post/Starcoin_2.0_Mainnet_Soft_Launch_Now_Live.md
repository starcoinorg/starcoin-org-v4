# **Starcoin 2.0 Mainnet Soft Launch Now Live**
**Introducing "Tempus"—The Future of Scalable PoW Networks**

Over the past three years, **Starcoin** has established a strong foundation for **blockchain decentralization and security** with its **enhanced PoW mechanism and Move language**. 

Now, we are thrilled to announce that **Starcoin 2.0** has officially entered its **trial phase**, marking a major step towards **scalability and commercial applications**. This milestone reflects our commitment to continuous innovation and excellence.

## **Starcoin 2.0: A Scalable PoW Network**
**Starcoin 2.0**, known as **“Tempus”**, is a groundbreaking upgrade designed to **overcome PoW scalability limitations** while maintaining decentralization.

With the **FlexiDAG** and **TurboSTM** parallelization upgrades, the trial environment achieves:
- **Max BPS (Blocks Per Second):** **1 block/s**
- **Max Parallelism:** **2 blocks**
- **Max TPS (Transactions Per Second):** **130,000 tx/s**

This ensures **fast transactions, low fees, and high efficiency**, solving issues of **network congestion and high costs** for Web3 applications.
