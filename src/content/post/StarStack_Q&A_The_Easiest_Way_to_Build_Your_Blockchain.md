## General Questions

**1. What is StarStack?**  
StarStack is a blockchain-building technology designed to simplify the creation of custom blockchain networks. Built on Starcoin’s years of experience in blockchain development and operation, StarStack combines modular architecture, template-based configuration, and automated deployment to streamline the entire process. Leveraging insights from real-world implementations and the challenges of building scalable and secure networks, StarStack represents a next-generation approach to blockchain infrastructure, making it accessible to developers and organizations of all sizes.

**2. Who can use StarStack?**  
StarStack is designed for developers of all levels—from blockchain beginners to experienced professionals. Its user-friendly tools and extensive documentation make it accessible even for small teams or startups.

**3. What industries can benefit from StarStack?**  
StarStack is suitable for a wide range of industries, including decentralized finance (DeFi), decentralized exchanges (DEX), GameFi, tokenized ecosystems, and stablecoin projects.

## Technical Features

**1. Why did StarStack choose Move?**  
StarStack integrates the Move programming language due to its unparalleled focus on security, flexibility, and efficiency in smart contract development. Move’s resource-oriented design ensures assets are handled safely and prevents common vulnerabilities such as double-spending, making it ideal for high-stakes applications like DeFi and enterprise-grade blockchain solutions. By leveraging Move, StarStack empowers developers to create robust and reliable smart contracts with ease.

**2. How can assets built on StarStack move to other chains?**  
StarStack is actively developing cross-chain bridge support, which enables seamless asset transfers between StarStack-based chains and other blockchains. This ensures interoperability and extends the utility of assets beyond a single network. Additionally, clients using StarStack can achieve asset liquidity by listing their tokens on centralized exchanges (CEXs), making trading and utilization across ecosystems easier.

**3. What is the performance of chains built on StarStack?**  
Chains built with StarStack deliver high performance by leveraging advanced technologies like FlexiDAG and TurboSTM. These innovations enable transaction throughput of over 10,000 TPS (transactions per second), ensuring that StarStack-based chains can handle high transaction volumes with low latency—ideal for large-scale DeFi platforms, gaming applications, and tokenized ecosystems.

**4. What is modular architecture, and how does StarStack use it?**  
Modular architecture allows users to customize and configure essential blockchain components (such as consensus mechanisms, tokenomics, and governance) without building everything from scratch. StarStack provides pre-built modules that users can tailor to meet their specific needs.

**5. What consensus mechanisms does StarStack support?**  
StarStack supports multiple consensus mechanisms, including Proof of Work (PoW), Proof of Stake (PoS), and other customizable options.

**6. How does template-based configuration work?**  
Users can select pre-set templates designed for specific use cases (e.g., DeFi, GameFi) and modify core parameters like token settings, governance rules, and consensus types to quickly customize their blockchain.

**7. What makes StarStack’s deployment process unique?**  
StarStack offers automated deployment with one-click scripts, eliminating the need for manual configuration. This significantly reduces the time and effort required to launch a blockchain, ensuring a reliable and error-free setup.

**8. Is StarStack compatible with the Move programming language?**  
Yes, StarStack integrates the Move language to enhance smart contract functionality and reliability, providing a secure and efficient development environment.
