## 1. Low Transaction Fees: Removing Barriers to Adoption

High transaction fees remain a critical challenge for mainstream blockchain use. On platforms like Ethereum, network congestion can cause fees to skyrocket, making it impractical for scenarios such as **micropayments**, **gaming**, and **NFT** transactions that require frequent, low-cost operations.

- **Comparing Costs:**  
  - Ethereum mainnet often charges more than \$5 per transaction.  
  - Layer 2 solutions like Optimism still cost around \$0.18–\$0.20 per transaction.  

These fees hinder widespread adoption, especially for high-frequency use cases where the transaction cost should ideally be **less than \$0.01**.

### How Starcoin Keeps Fees Low

- **Optimized Consensus & Execution:**  
  By refining its consensus mechanism and enhancing blockchain efficiency, Starcoin significantly reduces gas fees. 
- **Economical Infrastructure:**  
  Users and developers can transact at a fraction of a cent, making Starcoin a viable choice for applications requiring frequent microtransactions.

In high-volume sectors like DeFi, NFTs, and gaming, low fees are essential to encourage broader user participation and sustain growth.

## 2. High TPS: Elevating User Experience and Application Capacity

The rising complexity of blockchain applications—from DeFi platforms to NFT marketplaces and gaming ecosystems—demands **high throughput** to maintain smooth user experiences. Traditional networks like Ethereum often struggle with **scalability**, leading to slow transaction confirmations and elevated costs.

### Starcoin’s High-Performance Backbone

- **FlexiDAG for Dynamic Consensus:**  
  Dynamically adjusts block time, difficulty, and rewards, accelerating block production and transaction confirmation. 
- **TurboSTM Parallel Processing:**  
  Implements multi-threaded transaction execution, enabling **up to 130,000 TPS**, ensuring that Starcoin can handle massive concurrency.

These capabilities allow Starcoin to remain **resilient under heavy load**, delivering quick confirmations and better user experiences across DeFi, gaming, and social dApps.
