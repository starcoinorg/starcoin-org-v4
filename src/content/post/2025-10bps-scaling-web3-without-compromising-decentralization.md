## Solving the Blockchain Trilemma

The blockchain trilemma has long challenged developers: scale too fast, and you risk compromising security or decentralization. Starcoin tackles this head-on with its 10 BPS architecture, producing a new block every 0.1 seconds to support up to 130,000 transactions per second (TPS). This scalability is powered by FlexiDAG, a Directed Acyclic Graph structure that optimizes block production, and TurboSTM, a parallel transaction execution system that boosts throughput without overloading the network. Unlike PoS blockchains, which can concentrate power among a few validators, Starcoin’s enhanced PoW leverages battle-tested cryptography to ensure every node has a voice, maintaining true decentralization.

For example, contrast Starcoin with centralized PoS chains, where a handful of staked validators control the network, creating potential points of failure or manipulation. Starcoin’s PoW, refined for efficiency, distributes validation across a wide network of miners, ensuring no single entity dominates. This commitment to decentralization, paired with 10 BPS scalability, makes Starcoin a trustless platform for the future of Web3.
