Dear Starcoin Community,

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new website, marking the beginning of the Starcoin 2.0 era. Despite recent challenges, we are moving forward resolutely, driving the next phase of our development.

Visit the new website: [Starcoin Website](https://starcoin.org)

## Moving Forward with Optimism

While addressing security issues, we have made significant progress with industry-leading parallelization technology, greatly enhancing the mainnet’s TPS and performance. Starcoin 2.0 represents not only a technical breakthrough but also our unwavering pursuit of excellence.

## New Visual Concept

Our new website design integrates elements of water, light, and electricity, reflecting Starcoin’s unique identity and vibrancy:
- **Water:** Symbolizes flow and integration.
- **Light:** Represents our hopeful, forward-looking vision.
- **Electricity:** Implies the depth and technological significance of PoW mining.

## Core Advances of Starcoin 2.0

Starcoin 2.0 has made several technological advancements, significantly enhancing the platform’s performance and scalability. Key innovations include:
- **Enhanced PoW Consensus Mechanism:** Further improves security and resistance to attacks.
- **Move Smart Contracts:** Provide greater flexibility and programmability, suitable for complex applications.
- **FlexiDAG Technology:** Improves transaction concurrency with a flexible directed acyclic graph structure.
- **TurboSTM:** Significantly boosts transaction processing speed, ensuring efficient operation even under high transaction volumes.

## New Website, New Experience

The new website is not only aesthetically pleasing but also conveys our goals and vision for the 2.0 phase. We hope this update demonstrates our determination to innovate and grow even in the face of adversity.

## Unwavering Commitment, Building the Future Together

We sincerely thank our community for their support and understanding. Despite the challenges, we remain confident and committed to providing a better experience for everyone. Please visit our new website to learn more about Starcoin 2.0.

Let’s witness the future together!
