### A Greener Take on Proof-of-Work
Traditional PoW blockchains, like Bitcoin, are often criticized for their high energy consumption due to intensive computational requirements. Starcoin flips this narrative with its enhanced PoW consensus, optimized for efficiency through its 10 BPS architecture. By producing a new block every 0.1 seconds, Starcoin maximizes transaction throughput, ensuring more transactions are processed per unit of energy expended. The integration of FlexiDAG, a Directed Acyclic Graph structure, and TurboSTM, a parallel transaction execution system, streamlines block production and validation, reducing redundant computations and slashing energy waste.

Imagine a blockchain where every transaction is confirmed swiftly with minimal environmental impact. Starcoin’s 10 BPS makes this possible, offering a greener alternative that maintains the security and decentralization of PoW while aligning with global sustainability goals.
