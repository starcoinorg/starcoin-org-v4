What if a single Layer 1 blockchain could handle **130,000 transactions per second**, secure billions in assets, and power the next era of Web3 — all without the complexities of Layer 2 solutions? **Starcoin’s Modular L1** makes this vision a reality, tackling blockchain scalability head-on.

## The Scalability Imperative for Web3

Traditional Layer 1 blockchains like Bitcoin and Ethereum were designed with a focus on security and decentralization, limiting them to just 7–15 TPS. These constraints have created bottlenecks that hinder mass adoption. While many networks resort to Layer 2 solutions, these often introduce complexity, delayed finality, and additional costs.

## Starcoin’s L1 Breakthroughs: Modular Power Meets Move

Starcoin is the first modular, high-performance Proof-of-Work blockchain built on **Move**. Its innovative design combines multiple breakthrough technologies:

### FlexiDAG + PoW
- **Parallel Block Processing:**  
  FlexiDAG integrates with PoW to enable multiple lanes for transaction processing. In testing, this innovation has achieved over **130,000 TPS**.
- **Optimized Block Generation:**  
  The network supports a maximum block processing speed of 8 blocks per second with a parallelism capacity of up to 16 blocks, ensuring high throughput without sacrificing decentralization.

### TurboSTM + Move
- **Parallel Smart Contract Execution:**  
  TurboSTM enables simultaneous execution of smart contracts, eliminating bottlenecks during peak demand and delivering near-instant transaction speeds.
- **Enhanced Security:**  
  Paired with Move’s resource-oriented design, TurboSTM prevents vulnerabilities like reentrancy attacks, ensuring robust, secure smart contract performance.

### Modular Architecture
- **Dynamic Resource Allocation:**  
  Starcoin’s modular design allows independent blockchain components (governance, consensus, economic models) to scale dynamically without external overhead.
- **Simplicity and Flexibility:**  
  This architecture enables developers to build complex, high-performance dApps — from decentralized finance and gaming to real-world asset tokenization — all on a single, scalable L1.

## Building Web3’s Scalable Future

By solving scalability at the base layer, Starcoin eliminates the need for L2 solutions, offering:
- **No L2 Complexity:**  
  Users and developers avoid the risks and costs associated with bridging assets between layers.
- **Developer Freedom:**  
  Build high-performance decentralized applications directly on L1 with minimal optimization required.
- **Superior User Experience:**  
  Fast, affordable transactions drive broad adoption, enabling use cases from high-frequency gaming to expansive DeFi platforms.

Starcoin’s Modular L1, built on the secure and flexible Move ecosystem, sets a new benchmark in blockchain scalability. Achieving **130,000 TPS** through innovations like FlexiDAG and TurboSTM, Starcoin delivers the performance, security, and decentralization needed to power Web3’s next era.
