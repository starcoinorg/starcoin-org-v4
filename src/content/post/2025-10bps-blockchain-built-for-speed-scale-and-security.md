### The Technology Behind Starcoin’s Breakthrough

Starcoin’s 10 BPS capability is driven by a unique technical architecture. Unlike traditional blockchains that process one block at a time, Starcoin leverages a **Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)** structure, enabling the concurrent creation and validation of multiple blocks. Think of it as a multi-lane highway — each lane processes transactions simultaneously, massively increasing throughput.

Additionally, Starcoin applies **data pruning** to keep the chain lightweight and efficient, and implements **adaptive uncle block inclusion** to utilize otherwise orphaned blocks — enhancing both throughput and fairness.

Most importantly, Starcoin adopts a **latency-sensitive mining mechanism**, optimizing block propagation to reduce confirmation times. This makes it especially suitable for time-sensitive applications like DeFi and GameFi.

These innovations combine to create a blockchain that is not only fast, but smart — ready to meet real-world demands.
