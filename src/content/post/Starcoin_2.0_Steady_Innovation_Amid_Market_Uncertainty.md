As of August 2024, the global financial markets, including the blockchain sector, are experiencing a phase of sluggishness and uncertainty. While not as severe as the “crypto winter” of the past, the current market conditions have left many investors and users questioning the future of digital assets. Amidst this uncertainty, it becomes crucial to identify projects that continue to innovate and build for the long term, rather than merely surviving the downturn.

## Starcoin’s Commitment to Long-Term Value

At Starcoin, our journey through these challenging market conditions has only strengthened our commitment to delivering long-term value. The current market may be sluggish, but our resolve to advance blockchain technology — particularly in the realms of Proof-of-Work (PoW) and the Move programming language — remains unwavering.

In this period of uncertainty, Starcoin has not only persisted but thrived, launching innovative features that push the boundaries of what PoW chains can achieve. The release of Starcoin 2.0 marks a significant milestone in our development, demonstrating that even in challenging times, we are committed to continuous improvement and technological advancement.

## Starcoin 2.0: A Leap Forward in Blockchain Performance

Starcoin 2.0 is a testament to our dedication to long-term growth. This phase introduces two groundbreaking technologies: FlexiDAG and TurboSTM. Together, these systems have transformed our blockchain’s capabilities, achieving a 10x increase in block production speed and a 17x boost in transaction processing capacity.

In rigorous testing environments, Starcoin 2.0 recorded impressive performance metrics, with a maximum TPS of 130,000 transactions per second and a block production speed of 0.8 blocks per second. These results showcase our ability to innovate and adapt, ensuring that our development trajectory remains strong despite the market’s challenges.
